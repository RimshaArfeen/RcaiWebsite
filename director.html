NCAI Lab Director Dr. Muhammad Khurram

Director

Dr. Muhammad Khurram is a Professor at the Ned University of Engineering & Technology and also serves as Co-chair of the Computer and Information Systems Engineering Department. In addition, he holds a regional directorate position in the National Center for Artificial Intelligence (NCAI). In addition to being a Principal Investigator of Smart City Lab, an NCAI-affiliated research center, he is also serving as a director of the Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (RCAI). Prior to that, he was the regional head of the Kosher Foundation, a non-profit organisation based in the United States. His expertise and contributions are in the areas of AI, IoT, computer vision, on-chip bio-systems, embedded systems, digital signal processing, and VLSI system design.

He has 18+ years of academic experience with leading various R&D projects. Muhammad Khurram has Produced two Phd graduates and currently Supervising Four (02) PhD Students in the field of On-chip biological system modelling, neuromorphic hardware design and 5G Networks. As well Supervising various research-based projects at postgraduate and undergraduate levels. He has earned his Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer and Information Systems from NED University of Engineering & Technology. Ph.D. in science from Massey University in New Zealand.

Funded Projects
  • Won funding of Rs. 170 Million for establishing two affiliated labs of National Center of Artificial Intelligence at NEDUET
  • Won funding of Rs. 5Million, a Technology Development Funding Grant from HEC

  • Won funding worth Euros 100000/- for the project “Water Resource Management for Agricultural Areas of Pakistan” from DAAD – Germany under ‘German-Pakistani Research Collaboration Programme’
  • Won funding worth Rs375000/- for the project “Pakistan Student Satellite Programme” from SUPARCO
Honors and Awards
  • Founder of Aqua Agro , a leading agri-data company in Pakistan to address water resource management and smart irrigation through Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things. The startup company was incubated and graduated from the 1st batch of the National Incubation Center, Karachi. The company has been incorporated with SECP in 2018 and now in business.
  • Head of IEEE student branch of NED University and take care of different activities of this student branch related to research, development and training.

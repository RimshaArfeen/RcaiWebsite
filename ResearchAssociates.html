Imtiaz ul Hasan

Research Associate

Imtiaz ul Hasan is expert in Artificial Intelligence technologies with more than three years of experience. He has developed and deployed state of the art algorithms for solving complex problems in Healthcare, Agriculture and Environment. His research focuses on machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, object detection and semantic technologies. Imtiaz is currently pursuing a Masters degree in Artificial intelligence from NED UET karachi. He received bachelor's degree in computer science at the University of Engineering and Technology ,Peshawar from 2015 to 2019.

Weed detection algorithm for weeding robot:   Training and testing of object detection model, using dataset of 18000 images of weed and crops

Steering prediction using CARLA:   Built a custom Model for steering prediction using Keras

Load forecasting using integrated model:   Training and evaluation of different machine learning models on the Smart Grid data including Random Forest, Support Vector Machine , Naive Bayes and KNN.

Object detection for Autonomous vehicle   Training and testing YOLO v5 model for detection of pedestrian, cars and bikes

Theme prediction using NLP:   Theme classification of text from social media sites in four into different classes.

Pet bottle detection for reverse vending machine:   Trained MobileNet using transfer learning to classify bottles into 3 different classes including small, big, and nonbottles objects.

Azmat Hussain

Research Associate

Azmat Hussain is an expert Engineer with a demonstrated experience of three years in the embedded systems programming , development of IoT devices, hardware assembly, PCB-CAD design, fabrication, Robotics, and CAD/CAM. He also has experience in conducting various Robotics and IoT workshops. He holds a diploma in PRINCIPLES AND APPLICATION OF DIGITAL FABRICATION where he developed his skills in the areas such as Machine Design, Embedded Programming, Internet of Things, PCB CAD and Fabrication, 3d Modelling and Printing, Moulding and Casting and Composite Materials. He graduated from Sukkur IBA University in 2018 holding a bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering.

RoboMan:   He worked on developing a low-cost humanoid robot using the InMoov development kit.

Weedbot:   He used CAD/CAM services for the mechanical design of autonomous weeding robots. He utilised ROS Gazebo Simulation software for real-time simulation of the designed prototype.

IoT based water resource management System:   He played an active role in Designing , developing and upgrading the system on–chip devices and internet of things electronics.

Reverse Vending Machine (RVM):   He was actively involved in the development of hardware design of the machine.

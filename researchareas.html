National Solutions

PRECISION AGRICULTURE

We have introduced the concept of sustainable and autonomous farms as an attempt to deal with the growing demand for food caused due to increasing population. Our focused technologies include robotic weed control, soil probes, satellite imagery, smart irrigation, and disease detection to make farming sustainable.

SMART HEALTHCARE

Artificial Intelligence is serving the purpose to enhance and complement the very interaction of health care providers and patients through its applications in decision support, image analyses and patient triage. Our goal is to develop efficient disease prediction and decision support systems through patient MRI or Xray scans.

ROBOTICS

Robots are being widely used for various applications including security, space exploration, entertainment, agriculture and healthcare. We are currently developing weed-removing robots and humanoid robots as part of our efforts to offer robotic solutions in agricultural and service sectors.

INTELLIGENT-CYBER-PHYSICAL-SYSTEMS
(IoT)

Our IoT solutions are designed to accurately gather, display, evaluate, and store the data you choose to receive from your surroundings. We intend to take the lead in developing cutting-edge IoT solutions that enhance people's lives globally.We have developed smart IoT devices for Home Automation, industrial monitoring and smart indoor navigation systems.

INTELLIGENT TRANSPORT

AI is transforming the transportation industry by promoting safety through autonomous vehicles, congestion control through AI based traffic management and delay prediction, and organisational efficiency through fleet management. Our scientists are working to develop advanced driving assistance (ADAS) devices compatible with local vehicles to assist the driver during driving, and thereby improve drivers’ performance.

SOCIAL & ENVIRONMENT INNOVATION

At their core, ‘AI for Social Good’ projects use artificial intelligence (AI) technologies to positively impact the planet – and they span most, of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).We are focused on the applications of state of the art technologies to promote productive and healthier workspaces, cleaner environment, and capacity building of farming communities and youth.
